About Motor Torque Calculator

TorCal is a free online motor torque calculator that helps engineers, students, and technicians quickly determine the torque of a motor using power and rotational speed. The tool uses the standard mechanical engineering torque formula.

Torque Formula

Torque (Nm) = (Power × 60) / (2π × RPM)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is motor torque?

Motor torque is the rotational force generated by a motor to rotate a shaft or mechanical system.

What units are used in torque calculation?

Torque is usually measured in Newton-meters (Nm).

How do you calculate torque?

Torque can be calculated using motor power and rotational speed (RPM).
